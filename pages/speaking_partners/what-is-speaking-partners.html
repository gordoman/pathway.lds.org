Speaking Partners

What is the Speaking Partner Program?

The Speaking Partner Program is an integral part of the Pathway experience. Students in Pathwayâ€™s L version benefit by practicing and improving their English speaking abilities, while those in the Standard version benefit by being speaking partners and therefore assisting non-native English speaking Pathway students. Through the Speaking Partner Program, Pathway students enhance their English skills, increase in confidence, and have better job opportunities as they graduate.

Watch the video below to learn more about the Speaking Partner Program.

To watch more videos or learn more about Pathway, visit our
Media Page, or click on one of the social media links at the bottom of the page.

Requirements to serve as a Pathway English Speaker:

  • Speak native or near-native English
  • Be a Latter-day Saint or BYU-Idaho student
  • Be 18 years of age or older
  • Have access to Internet and video chat setup
  • Be available 30 minutes a week for 12 weeks
