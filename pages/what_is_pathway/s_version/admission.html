Admission Requirements

To be eligible to participate in Pathway, applicants must:

  • Be a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter–day Saints
  • Attend the weekly gathering during Pathway (one full year)
  • Strive to live by the BYU-Idaho Honor Code
  • Be proficient in listening to, speaking, reading, and writing English (proficiency exam may be required)
  • Be capable of entry-level college academic work with post-secondary disability accommodations, if qualified and required.
  • Be able to access a computer with high-speedinternet on a daily basis
  • Live within a reasonable commuting distance of a Pathway site .
Click here to search for a Pathway location/site near you.

The following are NOT required to participate in Pathway:

  • NO application fee
  • NO high school diploma or GED (U.S. & Canada Only)*
  • NO standardized scores (ACT/SAT)
  • NO ecclesiastical endorsement+
* High school completion is required for international students and for U.S. students who desire to apply for federal financial aid at the completion of Pathway.

+ An ecclesiastical endorsement is required at the end of Pathway for those students who choose to continue to the BYU-Idaho Online Degree Program.
