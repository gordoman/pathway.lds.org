About Us

The Pathway Support Center assists current students through their Pathway experience. Our goal is to provide support, tools, and encouragement to help students succeed in Pathway and beyond.

Our Services

APPLICATION AND REGISTRATION QUESTIONS

If you have questions about the Pathway application or registration processes, we have the answers.

EXPLORE ACADEMIC OPPORTUNITIES

We help students explore the many different educational opportunities that are available during and after Pathway.

WEBSITE NAVIGATION

Can’t find what you’re looking for on the Pathway website? Contact the Pathway Support Center, and we’ll point you in the right direction.

PREPARE FOR MATRICULATION

Whether you decide to continue your degree at BYU-Idaho or at a local school, we are here to help you in the next step.

MOTIVATION / ENCOURAGEMENT

Feeling overwhelmed? Pathway Support Center representatives are only a call or email away with words of advice, motivation, and encouragement.

ANSWERS TO ADDITIONAL QUESTIONS

Contact us for any other questions you may have.

After Pathway

Your Pathway experience is only the first step of your academic journey. We encourage you to continue your education and matriculate (start toward your degree) at BYU-Idaho or any other institute of higher learning.
